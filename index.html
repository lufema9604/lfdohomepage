About Me

Hello, I am Luis Fernando Madrigal Ayala, currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Astrophysics at the Universidad de Guanajuato. My research focuses on the physical mechanisms and transformation of galaxies in clusters, specifically studying tidal forces and ram pressure stripping. I work under the supervision of Dr. Héctor Bravo Alfaro, and I am passionate about understanding how these processes shape the evolution of galaxies in different environments.
